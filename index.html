About Us

The EMO R’ URHOBO ASSOCIATION of Dallas-Fort Worth-Denton was formed to promote unity and foster the cultural traditions of the Urhobo people in the Dallas area. Our association is committed to promoting the social and cultural needs of its members.

Our Goals

  • Foster unity among the Urhobo people.
  • Promote social and cultural needs.
  • Assist needy members in accordance with association rules.
  • Preserve and promote Urhobo cultural traditions for future generations.

Membership

Who Can Join?

  • All persons of Urhobo origin by birth or marriage.
  • Custodians of Urhobo children.
  • Legally adopted children of Urhobo families aged 21 and above.

Membership Fees

  • One-time registration fee: $50
  • Monthly dues: $35 (includes life insurance)
  • Annual hosting dues: $245 (due by June 30)
